What we do

Coffee, brunch, and a welcome you stay for.

Three things we've cared about since the day we opened — and the reason locals walk past the chain on the corner to come in here instead.

Coffee, slow

Mambo coffee, brewed slow. Tielka tea by the pot. Vietnamese iced for the warm days.

Brunch, honest

Bacon rolls, eggs benedict, green eggs and ham, croissants out of the oven. Pesto we make ourselves.

A room you settle into

Antique tables, colourful cushions, a vintage stereogram you can spin yourself. The kind of room you don't want to leave in a hurry.

Menu

Brunch, all day.

The same menu printed on every table — bowls, bagels, burgers, pastry, and a full coffee program.

The space

A room you stay in longer than you meant to.

Antique tables, vintage stereogram, board games on the back shelf. Pull up a chair, pick a record, settle in for an afternoon with the regulars.
